We're Expanding: OneStop Launches Food Delivery Services Across Kenya — August 3, 2025

We're excited to announce that OneStop has officially launched operations in Kenya.
Our food delivery services are now available in Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, and Nakuru,
marking a major milestone in our mission to make quality meals more accessible across Africa.
Kenya represents a vibrant and growing market, and we're proud to bring our platform to cities
where demand for fast, reliable, and convenient food delivery continues to rise.
At OneStop, we believe in building strong partnerships with local restaurants and vendors.
Our expansion into Kenya reflects that commitment — giving customers more food options
while supporting businesses through improved access to digital tools and delivery logistics.
